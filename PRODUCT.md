# Product

Suppertime Helpdesk is an internal support tool for managing customer tickets from email and embedded web forms.

## MVP Users

- Super Admin: manages configuration and roles.
- Manager: oversees support queues and assignments.
- Agent: replies to customers, adds notes, and updates ticket state.

## MVP Capabilities

- Email-created tickets and threaded customer replies.
- Agent inbox, ticket detail, replies, internal notes, assignment, status, priority, tags, filters, and search.
- Public embedded support form with attachments.
- CSV import for historical Freshdesk data.

## Explicit Non-Goals For MVP

- Customer portal.
- Knowledge base.
- Live chat.
- Workflow automation.
- Multi-tenant billing.
- AI features.
